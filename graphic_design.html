Modernized "Western" Poster

–– Breakfast poster designed using Adobe Illustrator ––

For this poster, I began by searching the web (Pinterest, Google Images) for "Western posters". Once I had an understanding of the "Western" poster style, I brainstormed possible image elements in the poster (toast, bagels, eggs, milk) and decided that eggs would allow me to work with the three primary colors.

From there, I chose bold fonts, and used multiple drop shadows on the word "breakfast" to depict the "Western" style.

Mood Board

–– Mood Board created using Adobe Photoshop ––

My goal was to create a mood board was to create a logo, choose a color theme, choose a font-family, and use photos to depict my personal style if I had a design company. I needed to convey the styles and concepts that could be used for web development, social media profiles, and branding in general.

I began by developing a rough, image-based mood board on Pinterest to get an idea of what color scheme, aesthetic, and style I would use. Using many of the photos from the Pinterest board, I developed a color palette that I used throughout the mood board. Along with this, I developed a logo for my imaginary company, including a milk carton because I personally love milk and the way milk cartons look (personal branding!).
